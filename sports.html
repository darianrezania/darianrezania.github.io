The New England Patriots

Also half of my family lives in Boston and my constant trips there made me love the team even more. My uncle and friends and Massuchusetts influenced to like the team by making me watch the Patriots play. The team has just inspired and shown me that you should never give up even if the odds are 1000 to 1.
